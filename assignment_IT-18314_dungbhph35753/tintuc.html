The best Genshin Impact Ganyu build

Ganyu was introduced in the 1.2 update alongside the Dragonspine region, an icy location filled with plenty of frosty minions. Ganyu’s abilities work well against the enemies in this region, but you’ll find that Pyro characters fare significantly better against Cryo minions. She will be returning alongside Zhongli and others as part of a re-run banner due to go live on January 25, 2022, so keep an eye out for it.
